Eat-So-They-Can by Inventor91

Eat So They Can is an initiative of Global Volunteer Network, and Global Volunteer Network Foundation. The concept is simple - by hosting a dinner party, you can help end poverty around the world!

Support our Four Causes The funds raised through Eat So They Can will be used to support four causes. When you sign-up to be a host you will be given the opportunity to select which cause you would like your funds to support. We believe it is important that you feel connected and committed to your cause. Funds raised for this cause will be used towards projects working with orphaned and vulnerable children around the world. By supporting this cause you will be giving children an opportunity to have a brighter future. Your funds will be used to support both immediate needs and sustainability projects at schools and orphanages.
